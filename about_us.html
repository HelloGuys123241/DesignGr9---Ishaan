About The Creator

Welcome to our website! I am Ishaan Reddy the creator of this website as a part of Design e-Portfolio. I study in SNIS, Grade 9 our task was to promote tourism in a certain place. I chose Gold Coast because it is place that I really like and want to visit again, Another reason, I chose Gold Coast is through my research as I found out that the tibe in the area strugle economically and don't have access to proper education.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote tourism in Gold Coast as result of which will help the Aborginal Tribes living in that area sell their artworks, diversify their culture, knowledge and heritage, and also economically as well it can help them sustain their families by giving their children a good level of education in the future. I hope this website will help the Aborginal Tribes of Gold Coast, Australia and their culture and heritage to stay alive for a much longer time and not die like the many other tribes.
